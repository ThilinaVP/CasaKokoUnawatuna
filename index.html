THE EXPERIENCE

Beach Minutes Away

Welcome to Casa Koko Unawatuna, a welcoming villa just a 2-minute walk from the famous Unawatuna Beach. Designed for comfort and convenience, our property features air-conditioned double rooms, spacious hostel accommodation for groups, and inviting shared spaces including a cozy lobby and a rooftop balcony with scenic views. Wake up to a complimentary bed and breakfast, then step out to explore nearby cafes, restaurants, and coastal attractions. Whether you're traveling as a couple, family, or with friends, Casa Koko offers a relaxed stay close to the beach while maintaining a peaceful atmosphere.
